Galvanic Skin Response measures tiny changes in conductance as you respond to different stimuli. Traditionally used as a lie detector or in therapy sessions to ascertain the patients true feelings, the technique is based on increased secretions of microsweat during sympathetic arousal from areas with high densities of sweat glands. One of these areas is the tip of the finger which is why we suggest placing the sensor pads at the tip of the finger.

  1. Plug in your electrodes into the audio input jack.
  2. Adjust volume level and scale text entry box until the signal plot ranges between 0 and 1.
  3. Ask questions! Discover the truth! Sudden elevations will show where a person becomes excited or stressed.

The gauge on the second screen shows where you lie on a minimum and maximum scale. To calibrate take an at rest measurement and a calibrate measurement, and then select calibrate.
